# 新一代 Windows 体验秉持的三大原则

节选自 [***Introducing a new generation of Windows experiences***](https://blogs.windows.com/windowsexperience/2025/05/06/introducing-a-new-generation-of-windows-experiences/)

> 作者 | **Navjot Virk** (Windows Experiences 副总裁)
> 译者 | **Ling Gao** (Windows 商业 & 云冠军)

Windows has always been for doers. As we debut the newest Surface devices designed for a new generation, we’re also excited to share the next wave of Windows experiences to help you get more done. (Windows 始终为行动者而生。随着我们推出专为新一代 Surface 品牌设计的全新设备，我们同样很高兴向大家分享新一代 Windows 体验，助您取得更多成就。)

We believe that everyone should be empowered to achieve more on a Windows PC. That starts with exploring how we can make our experiences more intuitive, more accessible and ultimately more useful. (我们相信，每个人都能通过 Windows 设备成就不凡。这源于我们始终在探索如何让 Windows 的体验更直观、更便捷，乃至更实用。)

When I think about how we innovate on Windows, our team is driven by three principles: (我们的团队在思考如何在 Windows 中进行创新时，始终秉持三大原则：)

- **We believe technology should adapt to you, not the other way around.** As we continue to deliver new and unique experiences, we will increasingly seek to personalize features to your needs, with your permission, understanding your preferences to deliver a richer and more consistent experience. (**我们相信，技术应该适应用户，而不是反之。**随着我们不断提供新颖独特的体验，我们将持续探索根据您的需求来定制功能，并在征得用户同意后了解其偏好，从而提供更丰富、更一致的体验。)

- **We believe experiences should be meaningfully useful, and fundamentally trustworthy.** Our features are customer focused and purpose-driven, addressing the real problems users face most. Before we even build, our features undergo rigorous assessments for safety, security and privacy. (**我们认为，体验应该是真正有用的、值得信赖的。**我们的功能以客户为中心、以目标为导向，致力于解决用户所面对的实际问题。在构建任何功能前，都必须经过严格的安全性、保密性、隐私性评估。)

- **We seek to solve the hardest problems users face on their PC.** We are committed to going beyond incremental improvements to reimagine how Windows can empower people in new and groundbreaking ways on the devices they turn to when things matter most. (**我们力求解决用户在使用设备时遇到的最棘手的问题。**我们致力于超越循序渐进的改进，而是重新构想如何以全新的、开创性的方式让 Windows 在人们最需要帮助的时候通过设备为他们赋能。)
